Our Services

Business and technical consulting for the full spectrum of healthcare data challenges — backed by decades of real-world delivery.

What We Do

Deep Expertise. Practical Results.

Compeer can provide business and technical consulting services for Health Information Exchanges, State and federal government agencies, and healthcare providers and payers. Our consultants bring senior-level expertise and a track record of successful delivery on complex, high-stakes healthcare IT initiatives.

Compliance & Standards

Regulatory Compliance & Interoperability

Navigating ONC and CMS regulations is complex and ever-changing. Our team has deep familiarity with the full stack of healthcare interoperability standards and the regulatory landscape — helping organizations comply, connect, and compete.

  • ONC and CMS regulatory compliance guidance
  • FHIR, HL7, and EDI standards implementation
  • EDI billing and audit compliance services
  • Technical standards for clinical data interoperability
  • Prior authorization and data exchange workflows
HIE Consulting

Health Information Exchange Design & Implementation

We are recognized experts in building and implementing HIEs at the local, regional, and national level — and have designed and implemented HIEs across the United States and internationally. From procurement strategy to go-live, we guide the full lifecycle.

  • HIE architecture and design consulting
  • Procurement and vendor evaluation support
  • MPI, ESB, and CDR implementation
  • Provider-to-payer connectivity
  • State agency and federal system integration
Payer Services

Payer Consulting & Quality Measurement

Compeer has worked across Medicare, Medicaid, and commercial payer environments, with particular depth in clinical quality measurement. We help payers measure what matters — accurately and efficiently.

  • Clinical Quality Measure (CQM) strategy and reporting
  • CMS eCQM and NCQA HEDIS measure support
  • Claims data analysis and management
  • Public health data reporting
  • Performance measurement systems
Provider Services

Provider Connectivity & Reporting

From connecting large academic medical centers to Medicaid agencies to managing clinical data reporting to state and federal systems, we help providers navigate the technical demands of modern healthcare data exchange.

  • CMS and State Agency reporting
  • Clinical data exchange implementation
  • Medical records and claims data workflows
  • Medicare, Medicaid, VA, and SSA connectivity
  • Healthcare data analytics and BI
